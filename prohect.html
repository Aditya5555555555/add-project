Food Menu

Grilled Salmon with Quinoa and Roasted Vegetables

Lightly seasoned grilled salmon served over a bed of fluffy quinoa, packed with protein and fiber. Accompanied by roasted seasonal vegetables such as bell peppers, zucchini, and cherry tomatoes, this dish is rich in omega-3 fatty acids, vitamins, and antioxidants, providing a satisfying yet nutritious meal.

Price: ₹ 150

Chickpea and Avocado Salad

A vibrant mix of chickpeas, avocado, cherry tomatoes, cucumbers, and red onion, tossed with a lemon-tahini dressing. High in fiber, protein, and healthy fats, this salad is both refreshing and filling, ideal for a light yet nourishing meal. The creamy avocado pairs beautifully with the crunch of fresh veggies and zesty dressing.

Price: ₹ 550

Zucchini Noodles with Pesto and Cherry Tomatoes

Fresh zucchini noodles topped with homemade basil pesto, cherry tomatoes, and a sprinkle of pine nuts. This dish is low in carbs and high in flavor, offering a great alternative to traditional pasta. The fresh basil pesto adds a burst of flavor and heart-healthy fats, making it a perfect choice for a light yet satisfying meal.

Price: ₹ 430

Cauliflower Rice Stir-Fry with Tofu and Vegetables

Sautéed cauliflower rice with tofu, broccoli, carrots, and snap peas in a light garlic-ginger sauce. This dish is low in calories and carbohydrates, making it a nutrient-dense choice. The tofu adds a plant-based protein boost, while the variety of vegetables brings a mix of vitamins, minerals, and fiber.

Price: ₹ 600

Stuffed Bell Peppers with Turkey and Brown Rice

Bell peppers filled with lean ground turkey, brown rice, black beans, and diced vegetables, topped with a sprinkle of cheese and fresh herbs. Baked to perfection, this high-protein dish is packed with fiber, vitamins, and minerals, making it a wholesome, well-rounded meal.

Price: ₹ 250

Sweet Potato and Black Bean Tacos

Soft corn tortillas filled with roasted sweet potatoes, black beans, avocado, and fresh cilantro, drizzled with a lime crema. These tacos are high in fiber, healthy fats, and plant-based protein, making them a tasty, nutrient-rich choice. The sweet and savory flavor combination creates a unique twist on traditional tacos.

Price: ₹ 890
